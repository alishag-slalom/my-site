A

Hi, I'm Alisha — a designer who believes that the best work happens at the intersection of rigour and instinct. I come to every project with a genuine curiosity about the people it will reach, and a stubborn commitment to getting the details right.

My practice spans brand identity, digital product design, editorial layout, and art direction. Whether I'm building a visual system from scratch or refining an existing one, I'm always asking the same question: does this communicate, or does it just decorate?

When I'm not designing, you'll find me in a gallery, obsessing over type specimens, or searching for the perfect cup of coffee in a city I've never been to before.
